Comet Cupboard Inventory Tracking System

An inventory tracking system for Comet Cupboard, an on-campus food pantry at The University of Texas at Dallas

Project Background

Comet Cupboard is an on-campus food pantry located within the library of The University of Texas at Dallas. The Comet Cupboard is dedicated to fighting food insecurity among University of Texas at Dallas students and faculty. Comet Cupboardis manned by volunteers, most of whom are students, as well as a paid administrative team.


Student volunteers are tasked with processing donations of goods and helping customers checkout. The checkout process included counting each of the customer's items then manually entering the (often ambiguous) category the item belonged to into a Qualtrics form.


Administrative tasks were performed separately using CSV files generated by the Qualtrics forms.


Goals

  • Provide a solution which streamlines volunteer and administrative tasks

  • Implement a data page which administrators could leverage to visualize data from within the application

  • Provide a customer-facing checkout system, which could streamline the checkout process

  • Implement an SMS-based queue system to mitigate long lines outside Comet Cupboard
