Biography

I am a software developer from small, rural town outside of San Antonio, TX. I have worked on software projects including UT Dallas', on-campus food pantry, Comet Cupboard, as well as a makerspace in Plano, TheLab.ms. I have done research, leveraging machine learning regression techniques to model metal contacts to two-dimensional semiconductors and pioneering the path towards nanotransistor technology.


Outside of coding, I enjoy reading, watching movies at the Texas Theatre, and volunteering with my Alpha Phi Omega fraternity brothers in the Dallas-Fort-Worth Metroplex.


Please feel free to contact me if you would like to talk about software, volunteering, or even movies you think I should watch.
